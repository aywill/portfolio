Arshia Will

Hello World! My name is Arshia Will. I am currently a grad student at the University of Michigan School of Information studying Data Science/Data Analytics. I have 5 years of professional experience working with data in the finance, IT, Auto, and Staffing industry. I have experience with programming, analytics, and natural language processing. Check out my resume here!

One on my favorite things to do is travel! I've been fortunate to see many great places domestically and international. My most recent trip was to Barcelona, where I soaked up sunshine and ate a lot of meat and cheese. My favorite part of the trip was seeing Montserrat, a multi-peak mountain range 40 minutes outside the city. Beautiful views, cool mountain air, and a warm glass of xocolatl made for a perfect day!

I also love a good story! I've been an avid reader and movie addict most of my life. I love learning new things, to a point where I obsess over a topic and talk about it with whoever who listens. Part of the reasons I love data analysis so much is it gives me an oppurtunity to tell stories with data. Analyzing and exploring data to present to others may not sound fun to everyone, but I enjoy it!
